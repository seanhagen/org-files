** Better Thermostat
- have relays in heater closet
- "thermostat" is split into two parts, relay & thermometer
- temperature goes to home assistant, hass tells relay what to do


pros:
- thermometer can go anywhere, or have multiple
- no worrying about batteries, as can plug into power in heater closet

cons:
- have to put panel up over old thermostat locations
